The Tokyo Conference on Institutions, Law, and Development (Tokyo ILD) 2025

June 28, 2025 | Room M, GRIPS, Tokyo, Japan (Access and Maps)

About the Conference

This conference brings together leading international researchers, economists, legal scholars, and practitioners to explore the integration of evidence-based policy-making (EBPM) into judicial and institutional contexts. Showcasing cutting-edge methodologies such as text analysis and machine learning, it highlights how empirical research can inform real-world judicial and institutional reforms that promote economic development. The inaugural conference will be held at GRIPS in Tokyo, featuring distinguished economists and a sitting Supreme and High Court Justice from Pakistan.
